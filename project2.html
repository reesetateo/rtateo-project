This capstone project, undertaken as part of my Google Cloud Data Analytics certification, demonstrates a comprehensive analysis of financial data, focusing on key metrics such as cash flow, loan status, and borrower location. The project leveraged BigQuery for efficient data management and Looker for advanced data visualization.

The interactive dashboard features a variety of visualizations, shown in the image on the left, including single-value metrics, bar charts, tables, and pie charts, to offer a multifaceted view of the data. Users could drill down into specific regions and gain insights tailored to their geographic interests, seen in the image on the right.
